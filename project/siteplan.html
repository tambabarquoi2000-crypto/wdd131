Site Name: GoldGlobalX

This name represents the idea of starting a brand that has a target of reaching the whole word.In its early stage, it is going focus on strengthening trust between Liberian Dealers and international buyers since gold is a high value commodity.

Available Domain: GoldGlobalX.com

Purpose

The site provides a hub where local Liberian Gold Dealers meet international buyers for gold transaction purposes. It will contain a listing of international buyers and a listing of local verified dealers and a proper Ecrows system to handle payment securely and safely.

Scenarios

1. What is the most secure way to buy gold from Liberia?

2. What happens if a dealer sends me fake gold?
